================================

**Introduction**
---------------

The Nexus Organism is a complex, adaptive system comprising multiple components that interact to produce emergent behavior. As a new curator, this manual will guide you through the system's architecture, functionality, and current state.

**Codebase Structure**
---------------------

The Nexus Organism's codebase is organized into the following modules:

* `nexus_organism/core/`: Contains the core logic and functionality of the organism, including its basic operations and interfaces.
* `nexus_organism/agents/`: Houses the agent-based systems that interact with the environment and each other to produce complex behavior.
* `nexus_organism/memory/`: Manages the organism's knowledge and experience, storing and retrieving information as needed.
* `nexus_organism/motivation/`: Defines the organism's goals, desires, and motivations, driving its behavior and decision-making processes.
